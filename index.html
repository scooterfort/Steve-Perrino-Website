ABOUT

I help bands and artists make records. Primarily I work with bands under the “rock” and “metal” umbrellas. This often involves co-writing & arrangement, time in the studio recording the songs, and post-production like editing, mixing, and mastering. Some studios don’t take such a hands-on approach, but I like to make sure that everything that leaves my studio has a standard of quality. Not just from a technical standpoint but from an artistic one as well. I’d say I’m most known for my input in the writing process, and that when you come into the studio with me we aren’t going to settle for mediocrity. People often joke that my most uttered phrase is “let’s do that again,” haha.
